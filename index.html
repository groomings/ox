Discord Bot Commands

help
Shows all commands or a specific category list.
Usage: .help or .help fun
dm
Sends a private message to a user.
Usage: .dm @user Hello!

Repeats or mirrors what a user says.
Usage: .mimic @user

Deletes a bunch of messages quickly.
